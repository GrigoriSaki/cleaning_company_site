About Our Company

Schoonmaakbedrijf Dick van Laar & Zn has been providing professional cleaning services in the Apeldoorn region since 1965. As a family-run business with decades of experience, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, quality and long-term partnerships with our clients.

Our daily work includes cleaning offices, commercial buildings, shops and the common areas of residential complexes. We create clean and comfortable working and living environments for our clients.

We work with flexible schedules tailored to the needs of our customers and focus strongly on service, quality and clear communication.

Some of our clients have been working with us for more than 50 years.

Safety is a top priority in our work. All equipment such as ladders and aerial platforms is regularly inspected and certified to ensure safe and reliable service.

We are specialised in working at height using professional aerial platforms and certified equipment.

Since 1995 we have specialised in high-access cleaning using professional aerial platforms.
